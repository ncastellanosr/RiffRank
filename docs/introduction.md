## The Problem
Many times I (and a lot of other people too, surely), have tried to, somehow, rank, classify, compile my songs. But eventually, you listen to so many artists, which mean a lot of songs as well, that you are not able to manage that in a satisfying way (as a music amateur). That is the reason why I was inspired to create a platform that would simplify and extend that obsession easily for any user.

The platform should offer a superior ranking system, specialized in music (for the scope of this project, Metal Music), to solve common problems, such as indecision about relative vs absolute ranking, or making those rankings useful after they have become too big to be read.

The basic functionalities should be an easy-to-use ranking system, playlist recommendations, music taste analysis, among similar features. Social media and community features would be nice to add in the future.

As a summary, this project is about a music listening tracking system.
